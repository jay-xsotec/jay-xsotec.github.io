Our Story

An architecture-focused information systems start-up leveraging specialized engine frameworks to optimize institutional operations at scale.

Following extensive tenures analyzing institutional technical gaps with the School District of Philadelphia, founder Jay Cohen established xSoTec to deliver powerful, lower-overhead data options to schools and enterprises. By engineering proprietary backend script toolkits—including our main open-source assets adminConsole and copyScripts—our specialized team can instantly deploy micro-architectures that translate directly into massive operational efficiency, empowering users all over the world.
