Our Story

An information systems start-up using Google Scripts to provide schools and non-profits with accessible and low-cost data management.

Following years of work as an educational technology consultant with the School District of Philadelphia and surrounding area, founder Jay Cohen created an information systems start-up using Google Scripts to provide schools and non-profits with accessible and low-cost data management. With a small group of coders and testers, xSoTec spent years developing proprietary scripts that quickly can be applied to an organization, resulting in our two main open-source programs adminConsole and copyScripts. With these scripts, we work directly with schools to create products to help both students and educators around the world.

"Working with xSoTec to create our custom dashboard completely changed our lives when it comes to assessing and tracking student progress. Previous platforms forced us to change our practice and language to fit within a system... We were looking for something custom built for us." — Partner School District Administrator
